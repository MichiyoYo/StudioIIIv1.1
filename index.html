Trust your teeth to an expert 

Anacapa Dental Art Institute is considered one of the world leaders in dentistry. Located in luxurious Ventura County, Anacapa has established itself as a trendsetter in cutting edge dental surgery and dental impants. In fact, one would not be exaggerating if they called Anacapa’s Dr. Saj Jivraj one of the foremost opinion leaders in the field of cosmetic dentistry.

"Providing expert and compassionate

five-star dental service that has no rival."

Our staff is friendly and helpful, and we are here to help you achieve your dental goals. When you come in for your consultation, we will discuss your life and what you want out of your Anacapa experience. From there, our expert Dr. Jivraj will give you options as to how to accomplish what you want out of your experience. Nowhere along the process are you treated as anything less than the most important guest of our establishment.
